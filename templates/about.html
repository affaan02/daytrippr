About Tripski

Tripski is a laser-focused day-trip finder: arrive in the morning, return in the evening. It started as a student project to make quick getaways dead simple. This MVP uses mock data while we integrate live providers.

Fast

Two fields, one date, done. We do the filtering for you.

Opinionated

Morning arrival + evening return. No clutter, just day-trip logic.

Practical

Opens in Google Flights to book with your miles and status.
